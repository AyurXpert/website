About Us

Healthcare Technology for Ayurveda Practitioners

AyurXpert Technologies™ is a healthcare technology company developing digital health management software for Ayurveda healthcare providers across India and internationally. Our platform enables Ayurveda clinics, hospitals, and practitioners to securely manage patient health records and clinical workflows.

We are committed to building technology that respects patient privacy, ensures data security, and complies with India's national digital health standards under the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM).

Our solutions are designed to support Ayurveda healthcare organisations in their digital transformation journey — from patient registration to clinical documentation — with security and compliance at the core.

Our Objectives

What We Set Out to Achieve

AyurXpert Technologies™ is driven by a clear mission — to bring every Ayurveda healthcare provider into the digital age, securely and responsibly.

01
Digital Empowerment for Ayurveda
To make modern, secure, and cloud-based health information management accessible to every Ayurveda healthcare provider in India — from single-practitioner clinics to multi-branch hospitals — regardless of scale or location.
02
Honour Clinical Tradition
To develop technology that respects the depth of Ayurveda practice — enabling accurate digital documentation of traditional assessments, Panchakarma workflows, and classical treatment protocols.
03
National Digital Health Integration
To ensure every Ayurveda practitioner can participate fully in India's Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission — with ABHA-linked patient records, FHIR-compatible data, and seamless HIE connectivity.
04
Privacy, Security & Trust
To uphold the highest standards of patient data privacy and security — ensuring informed consent, transparent data policies, and full compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

Privacy

Data Protection Statement

AyurXpert Technologies™ is committed to protecting the privacy and security of patient health information. We collect and process health data solely for the purpose of providing healthcare management services to registered healthcare organisations.

All patient data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Access to patient records is strictly controlled — only authorised staff of the respective healthcare organisation can access their patients' data. No patient data is shared with third parties without explicit consent.

We comply with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the IT (Amendment) Act, 2008, and applicable provisions of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Our platform is designed to comply with NHA's Health Data Management Policy under the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission.

For any privacy-related queries, data deletion requests, or to report a concern, please contact us at privacy@ayurxpert.com. Full privacy policy available at ayurxpert.com/privacy.
