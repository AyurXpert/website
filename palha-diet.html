📖 Preparation SOP Reference
1Weigh coarse herb powder as per Matra.
2Soak in 16 parts water for 30 min (or overnight for harder roots).
3Boil uncovered on medium flame, stirring occasionally.
4Reduce to ¼ of original volume (4 parts from 16).
5Filter through clean muslin cloth while hot.
6Serve warm (not boiling). Add prescribed Anupana.
Use within 24 hrs · Reheat gently · Do not boil after preparation

2Combine: 1 part Kalka + 8 parts fresh milk + 8 parts water.
3Heat on low flame, stirring continuously.
4Continue until all water evaporates — only milk remains.
5Filter, allow to cool slightly, add sugar/mishri if prescribed.
6Serve warm. Ideal before sunrise (Brahma Muhurta).
