DPC Meeting Register — NCISM §6(2) Pharmacy Committee
NCISM §18at — Drug Procurement Committee: The DPC must meet quarterly (minimum) to review drug procurement, supplier performance, pricing, and formulary additions. Members should include: Medical Superintendent / Principal, Head of Pharmacognosy, Chief Pharmacist, and at least 2 departmental heads.
